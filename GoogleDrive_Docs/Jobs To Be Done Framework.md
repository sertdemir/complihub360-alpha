# **CompliHub360 — Jobs To Be Done Framework**

Version: 2.0

Audience: Product Strategy, UX, Marketing, Growth

---

# **Introduction**

The Jobs To Be Done (JTBD) framework describes **why users come to CompliHub360** and what progress they want to make in their situation.

Instead of focusing on features, JTBD focuses on:

• The user’s underlying problem

• The desired progress

• The context in which the problem appears

For CompliHub360, JTBD helps align:

* Wizard questions  
* Search experience  
* Results page architecture  
* Monetization

---

# **Core Job 1 — Expand Safely**

User Situation:

A company wants to enter a new country or market.

Job Statement:

“When expanding to a new country, I want to understand compliance obligations so that I can operate without legal risk.”

Functional Outcomes

• Identify mandatory registrations

• Understand regulatory requirements

• Determine necessary certifications

Emotional Outcomes

• Confidence in expansion decisions

• Reduced anxiety about legal risk

Wizard Questions Connected to This Job

• Where are you operating?

• What markets are involved?

• What product category do you sell?

Product Features Supporting This Job

• Market selection wizard

• Regulatory overview

• Compliance roadmap

• Provider matching

---

# **Core Job 2 — Validate Risk**

User Situation:

The company wants to launch a product, campaign, or service but is unsure about legal exposure.

Job Statement:

“When launching a campaign or product, I want to verify regulatory risks so that my company or client is protected.”

Functional Outcomes

• Risk classification

• Identification of legal constraints

• Confirmation of compliant actions

Emotional Outcomes

• Professional credibility

• Reduced liability concerns

Wizard Questions Connected to This Job

• Do you collect personal data?

• Do you use advertising claims?

• Are you selling regulated products?

Product Features Supporting This Job

• Risk scoring

• Compliance checklist

• Legal documentation references

# **Core Job 3 — Find Reliable Specialists**

User Situation:

The user knows they need professional help but does not know who to trust.

Job Statement:

“When I need compliance help, I want trusted specialists so that I avoid wasting time evaluating providers.”

Functional Outcomes

• Identify verified providers

• Compare service providers

• Establish contact efficiently

Emotional Outcomes

• Trust in provider selection

• Reduced uncertainty

Product Features Supporting This Job

• Verified provider directory

• Provider matching

• Structured engagement request

---

# **Core Job 4 — Research Before Acting**

User Situation:

Some users prefer research before contacting consultants.

Job Statement:

“When I explore compliance requirements, I want structured knowledge so that I can make informed decisions before contacting a provider.”

Functional Outcomes

• Understand laws

• Compare obligations across countries

• Access tutorials and guides

Emotional Outcomes

• Sense of control

• Preparedness

Product Features Supporting This Job

• Law database

• Guides and tutorials

• Knowledge hub

---

# **Core Job 5 — Maintain Compliance Over Time**

User Situation:

Compliance is not a one-time event. Companies must continuously maintain compliance.

Job Statement:

“When my company operates internationally, I want to monitor compliance obligations so that I avoid future regulatory violations.”

Functional Outcomes

• Track compliance tasks

• Monitor regulatory updates

• Maintain documentation

Emotional Outcomes

• Long-term confidence

• Operational stability

Product Features Supporting This Job

• Dashboard

• Compliance tracking

• Alerts and notifications

# **Job Map — Compliance Journey**

This describes the typical progression users experience.

1. Identify expansion or compliance challenge  
2. Research legal obligations  
3. Assess regulatory risk  
4. Identify specialists  
5. Engage provider  
6. Execute compliance actions  
7. Monitor compliance status

CompliHub360 supports **each step of this journey**.

# **Strategic Insight**

CompliHub360 succeeds if it consistently enables users to:

1. Understand compliance  
2. Evaluate risk  
3. Connect with experts

The product must never overwhelm users with legal complexity. Instead, it should translate compliance into **structured, actionable guidance**.
