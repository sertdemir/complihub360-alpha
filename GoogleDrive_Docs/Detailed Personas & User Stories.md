# **Persona U1 — The Cross-Border E-Commerce Operator**

## **Profile**

Name: Alex Weber

Age: 34

Role: Founder / Managing Director

Company: D2C E-Commerce Brand

Markets: DE → FR → IT → ES

Revenue: €1–5M annual

## **Context**

Alex sells physical products across multiple EU countries using Shopify and Amazon.

He is growing quickly but feels overwhelmed by:

* VAT thresholds  
* EPR obligations  
* Packaging laws  
* Advertising compliance  
* Data protection requirements

He does not have in-house legal counsel.

## **Core Pain Points**

* Unsure when VAT registration becomes mandatory  
* Confused by different packaging regulations per country  
* Afraid of fines  
* Unsure which provider to trust  
* Providers often respond slowly or vaguely

## **Behavioral Traits**

* Action-oriented  
* Wants clarity fast  
* Willing to pay for reliability  
* Does not want academic explanations

## **Goals**

* Know what is required  
* Avoid penalties  
* Find a reliable specialist quickly  
* Expand safely

## **What Success Looks Like**

* Clear risk classification  
* Trusted provider contact  
* Quick response  
* No compliance surprises

# **PART III — USER STORIES (Demand Side)**

---

## **U-Story 1 — Direct Search**

As an e-commerce operator

I want to search for “VAT registration Italy”

So that I can immediately see relevant providers and guidance.

Acceptance Criteria:

* Results page loads with providers \+ laws  
* Primary CTA visible

---

## **U-Story 2 — Guided Wizard**

As a founder expanding internationally

I want a guided wizard

So that I don’t miss important compliance dimensions.

Acceptance Criteria:

* Wizard adapts to selected country  
* Structured answers feed into results

---

## **U-Story 3 — Engagement Submission**

As a user

I want to send a structured message to a provider

So that I receive a relevant response.

Acceptance Criteria:

* EngagementRequest created  
* Confirmation shown

---

## **U-Story 4 — Track Engagement**

As a registered user

I want to see whether provider confirmed

So that I know when to expect response.

---

## **U-Story 5 — PDF Export**

As a research-oriented user

I want to export a PDF summary

So that I can share internally.

---

# **PART IV — PROVIDER STORIES (Supply Side)**

---

## **P-Story 1 — Confirm Engagement**

As a provider

I want to confirm receipt via one click

So that the user knows I am responsive.

Acceptance Criteria:

* Magic link works  
* Status updates

---

## **P-Story 2 — Reply Efficiently**

As a provider

I want to respond within structured context

So that I can reduce back-and-forth emails.

---

## **P-Story 3 — SLA Warning**

As a provider

I want to be notified before downgrade

So that I can maintain partner status.

---

## **P-Story 4 — Downgrade Transparency**

As a provider

I want clarity on why my status changed

So that I understand performance expectations.

---

# **PART V — Strategic Insight**

Demand Side:

* Needs clarity and speed.

Supply Side:

* Needs quality and predictability.

CompliHub360 succeeds only if it balances both.
